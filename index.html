WelCome Speech By Prof. Oby Lilian Orogbu

I would like to take a moment to thank everyone who helped me with my campaign. I especially want to thank the voters in House District Awka City who voted for me and placed their confidence in me to be their state representative. I am humbled and honored.
In March, when I announced my candidacy for a second time for this seat, a well thought-out plan was devised that would take us to victory on Election Day, regardless of the opponent. That plan included meeting the voters face-to-face, getting my message out on a personal level, and letting the voters see just who I am. I accomplished that by starting my door-to-door ground campaign on May 8, exactly six months from Election Day. When all was said and done, I had knocked on over 8,000 doors, walking the vast majority of the district twice. It obviously worked. To the voters of Awka City, a big thank you.
